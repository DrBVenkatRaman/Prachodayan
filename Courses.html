Courses Research About Us




Artificial Intelligence
Artificial intelligence is inculcating human intelligence into a machine. The fundamental techniques involved in making it is put forth in the course. It requires basic notion of graph theory, mathematical logic, data structures (trees and graph traversals). The student is expected to understand and have hands on experience on how the basic techniques of artificial intelligence works on a computing machine.




Neuro Computing
This course introduces the computation that goes in brain which makes it learn patterns. It covers fundamental concepts like activation functions, learning rules, back propagation and multilayer perceptron. It also gives us the notion of how convolutional and recurrent neural networks compute to learn patterns.




Natural Computing
The course is the study of fundamental concepts of Natural computing techniques. It is further divided into three sub areas namely nature inspired computing, natural synthesis and computing with natural material. Several concepts along with mathematical demonstrations are covered under these sub heads.






Quantum Computing
The course aims at throwing light on the computational aspects of atomic and sub atomic particles. Quantum computation fundamentals along with hands on experience with quantum gates would be explored. The working and analysis of quantum algorithms and a few quantum computer physical realization techniques would be covered.
