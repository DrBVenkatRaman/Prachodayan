Courses Research About Us




      Prachodayan aims at distinguished research at understanding natural sciences from computation's perspective. It tries to identify the operations that are involved right from atomic and sub-atomic level. It believes that the complex problems which are not solved by classical methodologies can be solved using these distinguished approaches. Indeed we began the research from machine learning projects and now we are heading towards the quantum way of representing information and learning. Various approaches for quantum machine learning have been studied and different architectures of quantum neural networks have been learnt as well as proposed by us. We have worked on various forms of data like images, audio, video, text and the relational data and are also finding out ways of representing and learning them in Nature inspired ways. Trying to study the genomic data is also a part of our work. Systhesis of natural phenomenon is an added spice to our research work. Given below are the projects undertaken by us.


Projects


1. Artificial Life - Ecosystem
It is a natural phenomenon synthesis project that gives a visualization and understanding of how the natural phenomenon works. It demonstrates how various physical, chemical and biological attributes function on living and non living things in an ecosystem. The project is developed using processing-js code.

2. Quantum Consciousness
The project investigates the creation of mind using artificial neurons and quantum principles. The mind in turn is expected to work with both internal and external consciousness. Python is the primary development platform used for this project.
